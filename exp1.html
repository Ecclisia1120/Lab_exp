Preparation for the Exam

1. Make a Study Plan

Organize your subjects and allocate time for each. Prioritize topics based on difficulty and importance.

2. Understand the Syllabus

Go through the entire syllabus and highlight important areas to focus on.

3. Practice Regularly

Revise daily and solve previous year question papers to gain confidence.

4. Take Breaks

Take short breaks to relax your mind and improve concentration.

5. Stay Healthy

Eat well, sleep enough, and avoid stress during exam time.

All the best for your exams!
